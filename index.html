Jenis Penerbangan

One way

A one-way ticket for a journey can only be used to travel in one direction and not for returning

Return

Also known as “return air tickets,” round-trip tickets are flights from and back to the same location of origin.

Multicities

A multi-city flight is an advanced airline ticket which allows you to travel across the world using multiple stops: greatly increasing the value of your trip by booking extra legs with stopovers in several different cities!

Kelas Tiket

Economy

With its unbeatable value, economy class is the smart choice for travelers who want to get the most out of their trip while staying within their means. Why pay more when you can enjoy all the benefits of modern air travel at a fraction of the cost?

Business

Business class is a big step up from economy, with wider, more comfortable seats that either recline or lie flat; better food, sometimes served on china; and, often, airport lounge access.
